What we do

One company, focused on the systems that quietly run our lives.

We design intelligent products for the categories most people already think about every day — and most software still ignores. Our work begins with food, but the philosophy is broader: clarity, calm, and better defaults.

01 / Principle

Calm by default

Our software shouldn't ask for attention. It should reduce the number of decisions someone has to make in a day, not add to them.

02 / Principle

Connected, not cluttered

Grocery, inventory, recipes, budget — they're one system in real life. We treat them as one system in software.

03 / Principle

Trustworthy intelligence

We build with the patience of a system company: explainable defaults, careful data, and decisions you can understand and override.

The problem

Everyday essentials are fragmented across a dozen disconnected tools.

People keep a grocery list in one app, recipes in another, prices in their head, and expiry dates on a sticker. Most of the friction in daily life isn't a single big problem — it's a thousand small ones that don't talk to each other.

  • ×The shopping list doesn't know what's already in the fridge.
  • ×The recipe app doesn't know what's about to expire.
  • ×The budget app doesn't see what got thrown away.
  • ×Households repeat the same decisions, week after week, with no memory.

Friddy A product by Everything Essential

Smart food, less waste, calmer kitchens.

Friddy is a food operating system for the modern household. It connects what you buy, what's in your kitchen, what's about to expire, and what to cook — into one calm, joined-up loop.

Download on the App Store See how it works
How it works

One loop, end to end — from grocery to leftover.

Most apps cover one stage of the kitchen. Friddy covers the whole arc, so each step makes the next one smarter. The fridge tells the list; the list tells the recipes; the recipes feed the reorder.

01

Grocery planning

Plan the week using what you actually have, eat, and like.

02

Smart shopping list

A single list that knows your kitchen, budget, and routine.

03

Purchase confirmation

Receipts, e-grocery, or a quick scan — Friddy keeps track.

04

Inventory update

The kitchen updates itself: pantry, fridge, freezer.

05

Expiry tracking

Nothing forgotten at the back of the shelf.

06

Recipe recommendations

Cook from what's in front of you, not what's online.

07

Reorder when needed

The staples come back on their own. You stay in control.
