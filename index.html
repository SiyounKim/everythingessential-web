Our thesis

The most important software lives where decisions repeat.

Food, budgeting, and household planning are not one-time tasks. They are daily loops with real consequences: waste, overspending, stress, and avoidable risk.

Everything Essential exists to make those loops easier. We combine useful AI, clean product design, and practical data so people can act quickly without giving up control.

First product

Friddy is an AI kitchen OS.

One daily system for what you have, what you should cook, what you should buy, and what needs attention next.
